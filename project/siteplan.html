Site Plan: Volleyball Hub

Site Name

Name: Volleyball Hub

This name was chosen because it reflects the central purpose of the website: to be a comprehensive hub for volleyball enthusiasts. It conveys clarity, focus, and relevance to the target audience.

Optional Domain Availability: volleyballhub.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of Volleyball Hub is to provide resources, knowledge, and inspiration to volleyball players, coaches, and fans. The site will offer:

  • Information about the rules, positions, and strategies of volleyball.
  • Tips and techniques for skill improvement.
  • Features about famous players and teams.
  • Special content for beginners to learn the basics.

Scenarios

Example questions the target audience might ask:

  • What are the key rules of volleyball for beginners?
  • Who are the top volleyball players in the world today?
  • How can I improve my serving and spiking skills?

Color Schema

  • #00A8E8 (Bright Blue): Used for navigation bar, headings, and buttons.
  • #F4F4F4 (Light Gray): Used for background and section highlights.
  • #333333 (Dark Gray): Used for text and footers.

Reasoning: The blue provides a vibrant and sporty energy, while the gray tones add balance and readability.

Typography

  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for headings and titles.
  • Open Sans (sans-serif): Used for body text and descriptions.

Wireframe

The wireframe below outlines the layout for the home page:

Mobile View

  • Header: Logo and hamburger menu for navigation.
  • Main Content: Welcome message and a single-column layout for sections.
  • Footer: Social media links and contact information.

Desktop View

  • Header: Logo and horizontal navigation menu.
  • Main Content: Two-column layout with a featured image on one side and text on the other.
  • Footer: Full-width with links, copyright, and contact form.

Conclusion

Volleyball Hub will be an engaging, informative, and visually appealing website, aimed at making volleyball knowledge accessible to a wider audience.
